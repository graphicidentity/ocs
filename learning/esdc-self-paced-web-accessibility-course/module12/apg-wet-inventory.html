APG patterns without dedicated roles and their WET equivalents

Some ARIA patterns, like Accordion and Disclosure, don't have dedicated roles. Their child elements use roles (like button, region).

Pattern APG examples WET examples
Disclosure Disclosure

Details/summary polyfill (expandable/collapsible content). Adds ARIA attributes for legacy browsers. Adds role="button" to summary (its inherent role) and aria-expanded. Lacks aria-controls.
Accordion Accordion

The Accordion option of the Toggle incorrectly mixes tab and tablist roles and nests details/summary inside the tab role. Very incorrect.
